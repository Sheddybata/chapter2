  (a) every citizen shall have equality of rights, obligations and opportunities before the law;

## The Nigerian Reality

Section 17 is the moral center of Chapter II. It speaks to dignity at work, equal opportunity, health, welfare, and protection for those who are most exposed to hardship.

## Adebayo's Commitment

The campaign should avoid vague compassion and instead name deliverables: primary healthcare, social insurance, labor dignity, disability inclusion, and public services that reach poor households.

## Action Angle

Invite supporters to adopt this section if they care about dignity, fairness, and a government that protects people before they fall through the cracks.
