## The Nigerian Reality

Section 16A is a major opportunity for the site because many citizens feel the failure of government most directly through food prices.

## Adebayo's Commitment

This page should connect farm security, rural roads, storage, credit, irrigation, and market access. Food security is not only agriculture; it is also safety, transport, trade, and household welfare.

## Action Angle

Use simple, shareable language: if food is constitutional, hunger is a governance failure.
